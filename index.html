About Us

RIVO water is committed to give its consumers more than assured purity and taste. RIVO water will go through stringent purification process including micron filteration, RO and ozonisation as stipulated by regulatory bodies and followed by other packaged drinking water products but its final product will be more than pure water. In addition to the stringent purification process RIVO will be using natural ecological elements to not only enhance quality of water but to impart healing power to it. RIVO will use natural catalyst in source water. Water quality is to be determined by not only absence of inorganic substances but also by nitrates management. Root cause of pollution is nitrates and other non-protein-nitrogen (NPN) compounds. Proteins can produce still higher NPN when nitrates accompany them. The conventional purification technologies focus on suppressing these inorganic elements within prescribed limits. Reverse Osmosis (R.O.) for example is seen as a technology to tackle the inorganic pollution. It produces low TDS permeate and also produces a reject stream that has higher pollution potential. Very few people may know that the R.O. permeate is, in fact, more corrosive and can pose health hazard. The increase in the corrosion is due to the fact that nitrates (being the smallest molecules) are allowed to a higher extent (6%) through the membranes, while other ions are allowed only 2-3%. Relative enrichment of nitrates thus makes the R.O. permeate, actually more hazardous. RIVO water therefore focusses on the nitrate management at the very source by using a patented product which copies natural process of purification. Conventional denitrification technique consumes organic food and oxygen, to produce CO2 and waste heat. Nature prefers another reaction, i.e., combining nitrates, CO2 and waste heat to produce organics and oxygen. Green plants use this reaction. It is a resource-generating mechanism, while conventional denitrification is a wasteful reaction. RIVO intends to use patented natural catalyst which functions similar to green plants in order to manage nitrates. Low-nitrate systems develop self-healing ability. RIVO will use naturally produced catalyst that continues to produce need-based amount of active oxygen and drives eco-logical reactions. RIVO water will therefore be low on nitrates and high on active oxygen. This active oxygen producing water further purified by stipulated purification process makes it unmatched in quality and imparts natural healing abilities to RIVO water. Endemics like swine flu , bird flu etc are caused by viruses. Each virus needs a bandwidth of nitrates. Only when our body goes into this high bandwidth, we get affected. RIVO water will be low on nitrates and long term consumption of this water will naturally enhance resistance to such diseases. Besides active oxygen producing abilities of this water will help in metabolism and augment good health. RIVO water will not only be pure but healthier than any competing product. It will have natural healing powers. Any competing water product is drawing water from a given source and processing it through conventional purification process which is common for all. RIVO differs in the treatment of water at the very source before it is further purified. The quality of input water itself is enhance greatly by lowering nitrate contents through a patented natural catalyst which emulates natural cleaning mechanism of plants. One gram of this natural catalyst has nitrate control abilities similar to 1 acre of rich forest. Nature knows best how to keep the water sources clean and nourishing and RIVO intends to leverage this power of nature to give its consumers pure, nourishing, healthy healing water. RIVO water is pure at birth. This is the best water that money can buy.
